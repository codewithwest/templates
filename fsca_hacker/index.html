How to Play

  1. A gamer is given a specified amount of money when starting the game and is tasked with paying a specified amount each time they land on a tour guide.

  2. When the amount of money a gamer has is depleted the game has been lost.

  3. The aim of the game is to survive while exploring the best that South Africa has to offer

  4. Roll the dice to move.
